How to play?


🕹️ Controls:

Move: W, A, S, D

Shoot: Space (aim with arrow keys)

Shift + move: Sprint

You will face hordes of zombies and must work together with your friends to survive. 🧠

How to create a room? 👥

/* Right box */


1. Click on the "Create Room" button.

2. Enter a room name and click "Create".

3. Share the room name with your friends.
