How to play?

Move around the character, by using the arrows.

Use Shift + A for specieal skill.

Work together as a team to survive the zombie waves

Upgrade hp and abilities in the shop

The fall of humanity!

It started as a secret experiment, a bioweapon designed to enhance human survival. But the virus mutated, spreading uncontrollably, and the infected didn’t stay dead. The city quickly descended into chaos. The quarantine failed, and panic spread like wildfire. The undead roam the streets, making these streets a wasteland. The key to survival is in your hands.
